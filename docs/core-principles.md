# Core Principles

REP-01 defines a reference model for owner-governed AI-facing business profiles.

## Epistemic Uncertainty

AI-mediated discovery systems may avoid citing a business when the available public signals are fragmented, contradictory, or difficult to verify. REP-01 provides a bounded public truth layer so that stable business facts can be consumed with clear authority and scope.

## Owner Authority

REP-01 depends on legitimate owner or operator authority. A profile is not conforming if it represents business facts without authorized control over those facts.

## Stable Truth vs. Live State

Stable business truth MAY be represented in a governed profile. Live commercial state MUST be handled through transactional handoff.

## Public Projection

The public surface MUST avoid exporting internal uncertainty as public authority. A conforming surface should expose authoritative fact, authoritative boundary, or no statement.

## Scenario Boundedness

Recommendation support is scenario-specific. A conforming profile SHOULD avoid broad claims when only bounded scenario support exists.
